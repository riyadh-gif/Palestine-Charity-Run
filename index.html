Welcome to CharityRun

Run With Purpose Save With Hearth

CharityRun is more than just a run it’s a movement for humanity. This event unites us in compassion and support for those impacted by the tragedy in Palestine. Every step taken is a stand for peace, hope, and healing. Join us, not just to race, but to make a real difference for those in need. Together, let’s run with purpose and show that humanity cares.

  • Safe Shelter

    Donations will help provide secure and comfortable shelters for families affected by the tragedy

  • Safe Water

    Funds will go toward ensuring access to clean, safe drinking water.

  • Medical Support

    Your contributions will aid in delivering necessary medical supplies and healthcare services.

  • Environment Aid

    Donations will also support efforts to restore and protect the local environment.

About Us

Why Choose Us

Step Forward for Hope and Humanity

Our mission is to bring relief, support, and hope to those affected by the tragedy in Palestine. Through collective compassion and active efforts, we aim to provide essential resources, build resilient communities, and stand together in solidarity for a brighter future. This is more than a race—it’s a step towards restoring lives and empowering change.

  • Emergency Food Aid

  • Access to Clean Water

  • Medical Assistance

  • Education Support

Our vision is a world where humanity comes together in times of crisis, fostering peace and resilience in communities affected by conflict. We strive to build a future where individuals have the resources, safety, and hope needed to thrive, united by compassion and support.

  • Global Solidarity

  • Community Resilience

  • Sustainable Growth

  • Empowered Futures
